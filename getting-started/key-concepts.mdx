This page explains the key concepts and terms you'll encounter while using Podifai.

### Option Set

An **Option Set** is a collection of custom options that you can apply to one or more products. It acts as a container for all the personalization choices you want to offer for a specific product or group of products.

### Option Type

An **Option Type** is the specific type of input field you use to collect information from your customers. Podifai offers a wide range of option types, including:

-   **Text Input**: For short text entries like names or messages.
-   **Color Swatch**: To let customers choose from a selection of colors.
-   **File Upload**: To allow customers to upload their own images or files.

### Conditional Logic

**Conditional Logic** allows you to show or hide options based on a customer's previous selections. This is useful for creating a dynamic and interactive customization experience.

### Dynamic Pricing

**Dynamic Pricing** enables you to add extra costs to your products based on the options a customer selects. This is perfect for offering premium customizations at an additional charge.
