Welcome to the Podifai Quick Start Guide! This guide will walk you through the essential steps to get your first product customizer live on your Shopify store.

## Step 1: Install Podifai

First, you need to install the Podifai app from the Shopify App Store.

1.  Go to the [Podifai App Store page](https://apps.shopify.com/podifai).
2.  Click the **Install** button.
3.  Follow the on-screen instructions to approve the installation.

![Install Podifai from the Shopify App Store](/images/screenshots/quick-start/install-app.png)
*Click the "Install" button on the Shopify App Store.*

## Step 2: Select a Product and Add Options

Once installed, you'll see your Shopify products listed in Podifai.

1.  From the Podifai **Products** page, browse your product list.
2.  Find the product you want to customize.
3.  Click the **Add Options** button next to the product.

![Select a product and click Add Options](/images/screenshots/quick-start/add-options.png)
*Click "Add Options" to open the customizer editor for that product.*

## Step 3: Configure Your Customizer

You'll be taken to the visual editor where you can build your customizer.

1.  Open the **Options** panel on the left sidebar.
2.  Click or drag an option type (e.g., Color Swatch, Text Input) to add it.
3.  Configure the option properties in the right sidebar.
4.  Click **Save** to save your customizer.

![Configure your customizer in the visual editor](/images/screenshots/quick-start/editor.png)
*Drag and drop options to build your customizer, then configure properties in the right panel.*

## Step 4: Activate the App

Finally, activate the Podifai app embed in your Shopify theme to make your customizer visible to customers.

1.  After saving, click **Next** to proceed to the activation page.
2.  Follow the instructions to enable the Podifai app embed in your Shopify theme.
3.  Once activated, your customizer will be live on your product page.

![Activate the app embed](/images/screenshots/quick-start/activate-app.png)
*Enable the app embed to make your customizer live on your store.*

<Tip>
You only need to activate the app embed once. After activation, all customized products will automatically display their customizers.
</Tip>

Congratulations! Your product customizer is now live on your Shopify store. Visit your product page to see it in action.
