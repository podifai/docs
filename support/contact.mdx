We're here to help! If you have any questions, issues, or feedback, please don't hesitate to reach out to our support team.

## How to Get Support

### Email Support

Send us an email at **[support@podifai.com](mailto:support@podifai.com)** and we'll get back to you as soon as possible.

**Response Times:**
-   Free Plan: Within 48 hours
-   Starter Plan: Within 24 hours (Priority Support)
-   Growth Plan: Within 12 hours (Priority Support)

### In-App Chat

If you're logged into your Podifai dashboard, you can use the in-app chat feature to get instant help from our support team.

### Feature Requests

Have an idea for a new feature? We'd love to hear it! Submit your feature requests through our [Feature Requests page](/support/feature-requests).

## Before You Contact Us

To help us assist you more quickly, please have the following information ready:

-   Your Shopify store URL
-   A description of the issue you're experiencing
-   Screenshots or screen recordings (if applicable)
-   Steps to reproduce the issue

## Common Issues

Before reaching out, check if your issue is covered in our [FAQ](/support/faq) or [Troubleshooting](/support/troubleshooting) guides.

## Onboarding Support

**Starter and Growth Plan customers** receive 1:1 onboarding support from our founder. We'll help you set up your first customizer and answer any questions you have about the app.
