## Technical Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Will Podifai slow down my website?">
    No. Podifai is optimized for performance with native form injection and CDN-delivered images. Your store will remain fast and responsive.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do custom options appear in orders?">
    All custom options selected by customers are automatically included in your Shopify order details. You'll see them in your admin panel, order confirmation emails, and packing slips.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I use Podifai with other Shopify apps?">
    Yes, Podifai is designed to work alongside other Shopify apps. However, if you're using another product options app, you may need to disable it to avoid conflicts.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does Podifai support multiple languages?">
    Multi-language support is currently in development. You can manually translate option labels, but automatic translation is coming soon.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Customization Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How many options can I add to a product?">
    There's no limit to the number of options you can add to a product. However, we recommend keeping it under 10-15 options for the best customer experience.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I charge extra for custom options?">
    Yes! Podifai supports dynamic pricing, allowing you to add extra charges for premium options, materials, or services.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I show/hide options based on customer selections?">
    Absolutely. Podifai's conditional logic feature allows you to create dynamic customizers that show or hide options based on previous selections.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can customers upload their own images?">
    Yes, the Image Upload option type allows customers to upload images or logos for custom printing or engraving.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Billing & Account Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I upgrade my plan?">
    You can upgrade your plan at any time from your Podifai dashboard. Go to Settings > Billing and select the plan you want to upgrade to.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I cancel my subscription anytime?">
    Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. Your customizers will remain active until the end of your current billing period.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do you offer refunds?">
    We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied with Podifai, contact our support team within 30 days of your purchase for a full refund.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
