## Advanced Customization

This category often requires advanced features such as:

**3D Configurators**: For complex products that benefit from 3D visualization (furniture, vehicles, equipment).

**Multi-step Customization**: Products with multiple customization stages that guide customers through the process.

**Technical Specifications**: Products requiring precise measurements, materials, or technical options.

**Complex Pricing**: Dynamic pricing based on multiple variables and configuration options.
