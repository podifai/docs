Podifai offers a library of pre-built templates designed for popular product categories. These templates come pre-configured with the most common options for each product type, allowing you to launch your customizer in minutes.

## Why Use Templates?

Templates save you time by providing:

-   Pre-configured option sets for common product types
-   Best-practice option combinations
-   Professional styling and layout
-   Proven customization flows

Simply choose a template, customize it to match your brand, and assign it to your products.

## How to Use a Template

1.  From your Podifai dashboard, navigate to **Templates**.
2.  Browse the available templates and select the one that fits your product type.
3.  Click **Use Template**.
4.  Customize the template to match your brand and product specifications.
5.  Assign the template to your products.

## Customizing Templates

All templates are fully customizable. You can:

-   Add or remove options
-   Change colors and fonts
-   Adjust pricing
-   Modify conditional logic
-   Rename options and values

## Creating Your Own Template

If you've built a customizer that you use frequently, you can save it as a custom template for future use. This is perfect for stores with multiple similar products.

## Template Best Practices

<Tip>
Start with a template that's closest to your product type, then customize it to fit your specific needs.
</Tip>

<Note>
Templates are a great starting point, but don't be afraid to experiment and make them your own!
</Note>
