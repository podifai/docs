## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I change plans at any time?">
    Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from your Podifai dashboard. Changes take effect immediately.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens if I exceed my plan limits?">
    If you exceed the number of custom products on your plan, you'll be prompted to upgrade. Your existing customizers will continue to work until you upgrade.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do you offer annual billing?">
    Currently, all plans are billed monthly. Annual billing with a discount is coming soon.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is there a free trial?">
    The Free Forever plan serves as our free trial. You can test all features with 1 custom product before deciding to upgrade.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
